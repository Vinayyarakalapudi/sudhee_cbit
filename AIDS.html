About INSIGHT

INSIGHT is the flagship event of the AIDS department, designed to bring together innovators and professionals in the field of AIDS research and awareness. This event aims to showcase the latest advancements and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their skills.

Join us for an exciting day filled with workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities!

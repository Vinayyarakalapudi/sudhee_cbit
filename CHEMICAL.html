About ChEMSPARK 2K25

ChEM-SPARK 2K25 is the flagship event of the Chemical Engineering department, scheduled for April 29, 2025. This event aims to foster innovation and collaboration among students and professionals in the field of chemical engineering.

Join us for an exciting day filled with workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities!

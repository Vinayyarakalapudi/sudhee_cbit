About ELECTRET 2K25

ELECTRET is the flagship event of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering department, designed to bring together innovators and professionals in the field of electrical engineering. This event aims to showcase the latest advancements and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their skills.

Join us for an exciting day filled with workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities!

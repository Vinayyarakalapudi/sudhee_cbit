About NEOZOIN

NEOZOIN is the flagship event of the Biotechnology department, designed to bring together innovators and researchers in the field of biotechnology. This event aims to showcase the latest advancements and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their skills.

Join us for an exciting day filled with workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities!

About SYNAPSE 2K25

SYNAPSE 2K25 is the flagship event of the Electronics and Communication Engineering department during the Sudhee festival. This event aims to bring together tech enthusiasts and innovators to showcase their skills and creativity in a competitive environment.

Join us for an exciting day filled with innovative challenges, networking opportunities, and fun!

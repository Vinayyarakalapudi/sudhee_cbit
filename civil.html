About CIVILIZATIONS 2K25

CIVILIZATIONS 2K25 is the flagship event of the Civil Engineering department, designed to bring together innovators and professionals in the field of civil engineering. This event aims to showcase the latest advancements and provide a platform for students to demonstrate their skills.

Join us for an exciting day filled with workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities!

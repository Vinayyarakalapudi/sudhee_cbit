About Sudhee

SUDHEE is the annual national-level technical festival organized by Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT). It features a variety of events such as hackathons, workshops, and competitions aimed at fostering innovation and creativity among students.

  • Event Type: National-level technical festival
  • Organized By: Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT)
  • Focus: Innovation and creativity in technology
